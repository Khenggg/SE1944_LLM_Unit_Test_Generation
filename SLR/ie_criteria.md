# Inclusion / Exclusion Criteria

## Inclusion Criteria

- IC1: Paper is written in English.
- IC2: Paper was published from 2018 onward.
- IC3: Paper is related to LLM/GPT/AI for test generation or unit test generation.
- IC4: Paper includes a clear experiment or benchmark.
- IC5: Paper includes metrics related to coverage, branch coverage, mutation score, pass rate, or test quality.
- IC6: Paper evaluates Java/Python code, functions, methods, classes, or projects.

## Exclusion Criteria

- EC1: Duplicate paper.
- EC2: Full text or PDF cannot be found.
- EC3: Paper focuses only on BDD/Gherkin/API/UI testing and is not related to unit test generation.
- EC4: Paper focuses only on test management or test execution, not test generation.
- EC5: Paper has no experimental data.
- EC6: Paper is not related to Software Engineering or testing.

## Screening Rule

If a paper is `UNSURE` in title/abstract screening, keep it for full-text screening.
